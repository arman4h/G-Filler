# Google Form Autofill Helper

## What is this? (in 5 seconds)
A lightweight Chrome extension that saves your common answers and fills Google Forms automatically.

## Why should I use it?
It saves time on repetitive form filling and keeps your frequently used responses ready in one place.

## What can it do?
- Save multiple field/value/tag rules.
- Auto-fill Google Forms based on matching tags.
- Edit or delete saved rules.
- Light/Dark theme toggle.

## How do I install it? (step-by-step, no assumptions)
1. Download or clone this project to your computer.
2. Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions.
3. Turn on "Developer mode" (top-right toggle).
4. Click "Load unpacked".
5. Select the project folder: e:\Personal\WebProjects\google-form-autofill.
6. The extension will appear in your extensions list.

## How do I use it?
1. Click the extension icon to open the popup.
2. Click "Add Data" and enter:
	- Field Name (for your reference)
	- Field Value (the text to insert)
	- Tags (comma-separated keywords that appear in the question)
3. Click "Save".
4. Open a Google Form.
5. Click "Auto Fillup" to fill matching fields.

## Is it safe?
Your data is stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage and is not sent anywhere.
